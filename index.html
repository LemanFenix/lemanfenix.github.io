Enhancing heat resilience for a cooler future

LemanFenix is dedicated to mitigating heat stress in poultry through innovative, natural solutions, improving animal welfare and securing food production in a warming world.

The Problem: Heat Stress in Poultry

Rising global temperatures are leading to a critical increase in poultry mortality, especially during transport. This poses a significant threat to animal welfare and the stability of food production.

  • Globally, 4.8 billion chickens are lost annually due to heat stress.
  • In Spain alone, this translates to an annual loss of CHF 24 million, with up to 60,000 birds lost per day during heatwaves.
  • The industry transports approximately 240 billion chickens per year, often in conditions where current solutions like ventilation and hydration fail at high temperatures and are not scalable.
  • Current legal mortality limits (e.g., 2%) are frequently breached during extreme heat events.

Our Innovative, Validated Solution

LemanFenix offers a groundbreaking approach that goes beyond merely managing heat stress – we aim to prevent it. Our solution, based on natural compounds, is designed to be:

  • Preventative: The first product to proactively build resilience against heat stress.
  • Effective: Works by reducing metabolism, improving thermal exchanges, and mitigating the physiological effects of heat stress.
  • Scalable & Cost-Effective: Easily administered via water or feed, making it suitable for large-scale operations.
  • Ecological & Regulatory-Friendly: Composed of natural compounds, facilitating a smoother regulatory pathway.

Unlike existing solutions that offer limited relief, LemanFenix targets the core biological responses to heat. Our approach is built on rigorous scientific research; we identify potent thermoregulatory compounds and validate their efficacy using established experimental models of heat stress.

Impact & Sustainability

LemanFenix is committed to making a positive impact on animal welfare, food security, and environmental sustainability.

Aligning with UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Our work directly contributes to:

  • SDG 2: Zero Hunger (by securing poultry production)
  • SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production (by reducing waste in the food chain)
  • SDG 13: Climate Action (by reducing CO₂ emissions associated with livestock losses)

Our Goal: To save over 1 billion chicken lives annually, which could lead to a CO₂ reduction of up to 5 million tons – equivalent to taking over 1 million cars off the road for a year.
